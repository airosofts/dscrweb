Brand System

The visual and verbal reference for every surface — marketing, app, and admin. Source of truth for color, type, components, and voice. Reuse these tokens when building pages; don't invent new ones.

Logo

01 — Identity

On Cream

On Ink

Do

Preserve the cream background square — the logo is designed as a bounded mark. Minimum size: 24px.

Don't

Recolor the brass accent, rotate the mark, or apply effects (shadow, glow, stroke). The system conveys itself.

Color

Voice

06 — Copywriting

Confident, concrete, and short. Copy earns its place. We do not use exclamation points, emoji in body copy, or hype ("revolutionary", "game-changer"). Action before benefit before reassurance.

On Brand
"Underwrite deals in seconds. Free, no account required."
Off Brand
"🚀 The world's most revolutionary DSCR tool — sign up now!"
